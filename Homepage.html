Breast Cancer Buddies

You are not alone in the journey to fight cancer!

We won this battle because we had friends who understood what we were going through. We had a community of survivors to back us up.

What we did not have was a website to facilitate such a community online. This is what Breast Cancer Buddies is.

We provide Stories of current patients and survivors, Tips and Tricks that everyone should know and a common Forum for everyone to chat about anything related to breast cancer.
